About WapiPay

Built for the routes people and businesses rely on.

Founded in Singapore in 2019, WapiPay was built to make cross-border payments feel simpler, faster, and more dependable. Today, WapiPay helps individuals, merchants, and businesses move money across trusted corridors in Africa, Asia, the United Kingdom, and the United States, with physical offices in Nairobi and Kampala.

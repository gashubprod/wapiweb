Privacy Policy

How payment data should be handled.

This page explains the categories of information WapiPay may process when customers use the website, app, business tools, developer services, and support channels.

Information We Process

  • Account, identity, business, beneficiary, and contact information.
  • Transaction details, payout references, corridor data, and support messages.
  • Technical data such as device, browser, usage, and security logs.

How It Is Used

  • To provide accounts, transfers, payouts, API access, support, and reconciliation.
  • To verify users, screen activity, secure access, and meet operational obligations.
  • To improve product performance, reliability, fraud controls, and customer support.

Choices & Contact

  • Customers may contact support about access, correction, or account questions.
  • Some records may need to be retained for legal, security, or transaction reasons.
  • Privacy questions can be sent to customercare@wapipay.com.

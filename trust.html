Trust & Security

Controls for serious payment movement.

WapiPay handles cross-border payment flows that require identity checks, route visibility, access controls, and operational monitoring. Product availability, settlement timing, and rail coverage can vary by market and customer profile.

Verification

  • Customer identity and business verification before supported movement begins.
  • Beneficiary and account checks where required by product or corridor.
  • Additional review for higher-value or higher-risk movements.

Monitoring

  • Transaction monitoring and screening controls for supported payment flows.
  • Operational review for flagged activity, unusual routes, or incomplete data.
  • Status and reference visibility for reconciliation and support handling.

Secure Access

  • Protected account access and role-aware business workflows.
  • API access designed around authentication, events, and payout status tracking.
  • Support paths for customer questions, transaction issues, and account review.

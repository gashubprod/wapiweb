Why WapiPay

Built to remove the usual drag in cross-border payments.

Most cross-border movement still breaks across pricing, visibility, and settlement. WapiPay is built to keep those parts together.

Typical setup
  • More intermediaries between sender and settlement
  • FX and fees spread across too many layers
  • Weak payout visibility once money is moving
  • Separate tools for retail, business, and treasury
With WapiPay
  • Local payout rails and route execution in one platform
  • Clearer pricing before money moves
  • Instant receiver payout across supported routes
  • Retail, enterprise, and OTC on the same system

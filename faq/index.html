Transfers and rates.

What is WapiPay?

WapiPay helps people and businesses move money across supported cross-border routes with rate, payout, and delivery information shown before confirmation.

Can I check the exchange rate before sending money?

Yes. WapiPay shows the rate, payout currency, delivery route, fees, and total debit before a transfer is confirmed.

Which corridors does WapiPay support?

WapiPay highlights supported routes across Africa, Asia, the United Kingdom, and the United States, including Kenya-origin and Uganda-origin corridors.

Explore WapiPay corridors

How do I send money with WapiPay?

Open the app, tap Send, choose the destination country, enter the amount and payout method, add or select the recipient, review the transfer, enter your PIN, and keep the receipt.

Business and developer support.

Can businesses use WapiPay?

Yes. Businesses can use WapiPay for supported payout, settlement, API, and OTC payment needs depending on corridor, product availability, and account profile.

Where can developers find WapiPay API help?

Developers can use the WapiPay developer documentation and API FAQ for sandbox testing, callbacks, transaction verification, and integration support.
